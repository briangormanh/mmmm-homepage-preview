Welcome

Welcome to our home

For more than thirty years, Chef Mauro Mafrici has cooked the food of his life, from Trieste to a Michelin-starred kitchen in New York to the heart of Chicago. One Magnificent Mile is the next chapter: an elegant Italian home at the corner of Oak and Michigan, where every guest is welcomed like an old friend.

This is not a restaurant group or a concept. It is one chef, one family, and one table set for you. We are grateful to be back in the city we love, and we cannot wait to have you at our table.

Mauro & Kimberly
Photograph — Mauro in the kitchen
The Chef

A lifetime in the kitchen, a homecoming in Chicago

Born in Trieste and cooking since the age of fourteen, Mauro earned a Michelin star in New York before bringing his celebrated restaurant Pelago to Chicago. After years away, he chose to come home, to the very first address on the Magnificent Mile, just across the street from where he and Kimberly live.

Every dish on the menu is a chapter from that journey.

The Menu

A menu that reads like a memoir

Authentic, ingredient-driven Italian cooking drawn from a lifetime of travel, from the seafood of the Adriatic to the kitchens of New York and Chicago. Some dishes are old friends carried from Pelago; others are written for this room. All of them are personal.
